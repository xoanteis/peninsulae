# Asset attribution

All assets in this game are real, human-made works downloaded from their public
releases. No AI-generated assets are used.

## 3D models — KayKit (Kay Lousberg) — CC0 1.0

- **KayKit Medieval Hexagon Pack 1.0** (`assets/models/hex/`) — hex terrain tiles,
  buildings, nature and props. Source: github.com/KayKit-Game-Assets/KayKit-Medieval-Hexagon-Pack-1.0
- **KayKit Character Pack: Adventurers 1.0** (`assets/models/characters/`, `assets/models/weapons/`) —
  rigged characters with 76 skeletal animations each, plus hand props.
  Source: github.com/KayKit-Game-Assets/KayKit-Character-Pack-Adventures-1.0

Licensed under CC0 1.0 Universal (see LICENSE.txt files alongside the models).
Assets by Kay Lousberg — kaylousberg.com. Credit not required, but happily given.

## Sound effects — Kenney (kenney.nl) — CC0 1.0

Files in `assets/audio/sfx/` come from Kenney's audio packs (RPG Audio,
Music Jingles, Digital Audio, UI Sounds, Interface Sounds), obtained via public
CC0 mirrors (github.com/iwenzhou/kenney, github.com/Calinou/kenney-interface-sounds)
and integrity-verified against the canonical pack file lists.
"Credit appreciated but not required." — thank you, Kenney.
